Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni is a former Indian cricketer and captain, known for his exceptional leadership skills and finishing ability in cricket. Born in Ranchi in 1981, he started his career as a ticket collector and rose to become one of the most iconic cricketers in Indian history.

Dhoni made his debut in December 2004 against Bangladesh. His aggressive batting style quickly caught attention, and his career took off after his 183* against Sri Lanka in 2005.

Beyond cricket, Dhoni holds an honorary rank of Lieutenant Colonel in the Indian Territorial Army. He is also a successful entrepreneur and has invested in sports, fitness, and agriculture.
